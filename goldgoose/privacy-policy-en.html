Gold Goose Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026

Welcome to Gold Goose. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: Email address for authentication and account management.
  • Financial & goal data: Savings goals, deposit/expense amounts, and asset/liability records you enter manually. We do not connect to banks or payment APIs.
  • User content: Notes on records and optional profile photos.
  • Identifiers: User ID generated by Supabase Auth.
  • On-device data: Local database storage for offline use.

2. How We Use Information

  • Core features: goals, records, progress, and cloud sync.
  • Local daily reminders (only when enabled; scheduled on-device, not ad push).
  • Security and troubleshooting.

3. Storage & Sync

Signed-in users' cloud data is hosted on Supabase (PostgreSQL + Storage), protected by Row Level Security so only you can access your data. A local SQLite database supports offline use and syncs when online.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with infrastructure providers (Supabase) to operate the service, or when required by law.

5. Tracking & Ads

Gold Goose has no ads, does not track you across other apps or websites, and does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

6. Retention & Deletion

We retain data while your account is active. You may delete your account and all data from Settings in the app. Deletion is permanent.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their information.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will be posted here with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? See our Support page.
